Kyle Scanlan. Laughter maker.

Kyle started doing comedy in Southern Illinois, which was somehow less glamorous than it sounds. He lives in Chicago and is a member of the Lincoln Lodge and the creator of The Whiskey Journal. He's performed at the Laugh Factory, the Big-Little Comedy Festival and etc. Previously he's helped run and produce showcases at Speak Easy Comedy, Wonder Bar Comedy and The Edge.

He regularly performs. Come watch him.
