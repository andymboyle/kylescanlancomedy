Kyle Scanlan. Laughter maker.

Kyle Scanlan is a Chicago-based comedian who started doing comedy in Carbondale, Illinois. Prior to stand up, Kyle was trained in both writing and improv at iO Chicago. Kyle has preformed at the Del Close Marathon, The Laugh Factory, Comedy-Bar, and different clubs throughout the mid-west. In 2005, Kyle made his TV debut on Fox's Stupid Behavior Caught on Tape. Kyle currently writes for the humor website The Whiskey Journal and is a cast member of both the Lincoln Lodge and Speakeasy Comedy. Kyle's brand of self-deprecating humor will leaving audience laughing... and wondering where his eyebrows are.
